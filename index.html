Our Café Menu

Espresso

A strong and concentrated coffee brewed by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Cappuccino

Espresso with steamed milk and a layer of frothy, foamed milk.

Latte

Espresso with a larger amount of steamed milk and a small amount of frothed milk on top.

Mocha

Espresso, steamed milk, chocolate, and whipped cream.

Tea

A variety of tea options available, including black, green, and herbal teas.
